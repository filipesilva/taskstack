Taskstack

A stack for tasks

Why taskstack?

Because whenever I'm working on something non-trivial, it feel like I'm pushing and popping subtasks out of a FILO stack. I figured a visual way to track it would help me focus.

Let's get shit done.

Click here to go back to the task stack. You can use enter to push and escape to pop, and your tasks will still be there if you close and reopen your browser.
